### Initial Release (v1.0.0)

Official launch of the Superinfo Apple MDM Professional Knowledge Base. This platform provides precise, field-tested Apple device management guidelines for IT administrators and educators in Taiwan.

#### Content & System Overview

* **Fully Bilingual Content**: Includes 220 professional glossary terms and 217 practical Q&A articles, all available in both Traditional Chinese and English.
* **Technical Baseline**: All content is built on the 2026 technical specifications, including iOS 26, macOS Tahoe, and DDM (Declarative Device Management) protocols.
* **Core Management Domains**:
  1. Account & Server Management
  2. Device Enrollment & Deployment
  3. Apps & VPP Management
  4. Apple Classroom & Teaching Tools
  5. MOE Digital Learning Project (Taiwan)
  6. Hardware Maintenance & Troubleshooting
  7. Advanced Mac Management
  8. Educational Practical Scenarios

#### Key System Modules

* **Dynamic Knowledge Engine**: High-performance SSG-based search engine with instant language toggling.
* **Professional Reporting**: Integrated issue reporting system in the footer that captures page context for efficient support.
* **Automated Maintenance**: Built-in scripts for Markdown linting and maintenance index generation to ensure content consistency.
